    01) there are 3 types of pizza which you can order: 
        01) olives, mashrooms, pineapple.

        01) when the ingrident for all types of pizza will ran out of stock the store will be closed and display message to the costumers saying that.

    03) the basic ingridient for all types of pizza: 
        01) 1/2 kilo flour.
        02) 1/2 Kilo cheese.
        03) 1/5 killo tomatos. 
    04) on top of the Pizza there will be one of the following topping: 
        01) olives pizza - 300 gram olives.
        02) mashrums pizza - 200 gram mashrums.
        03) pineaple pizza - 100 gram pineapple.

THE ORDER PAGE
    on the orders page a photo of the 3 types of pizza will be displayed, under each photo there will be the pizza price and the order button. 
    double clicking that button will create another order. 
    beneath the pizza photos there will be a current order list, displaying the pizza that was orderd and its price near it. 
    the total order price will be displayed int he bottom of the list, nearby a "confirm order" button.
    once the confirm order is pressed the ingridents of that order should be removed from the stock of inventory. 
    and a message shall be displayed to the customer with his order number. 
    
THE INVENTORY PAGE 
this page will display the current stock of inventory. 
and the entire orders history.
